Điền justification (ô "How will your app use this permission?")

DHTC Night Market is a traditional open-air market in Da Nang, Vietnam. We use pages_messaging to power an AI chatbot that automatically replies to customer Messenger messages. When a customer messages our Facebook Page, our backend receives the Messenger webhook event, processes the message through an AI model (OpenRouter / Claude Haiku), and sends a reply via the Send API within the 24-hour messaging window. Use case: Customer asks "What time does the market open?" → AI replies in seconds with accurate information → Customer feels attended to without waiting for staff. All messages are logged in our CRM for staff review. We strictly follow the 24-hour messaging policy — the app only sends automated replies in response to user-initiated messages, never cold outreach. A kill switch env var allows disabling automated replies instantly.
